We do not believe in a one fit all rigid syllabus. We offer personalized teaching to individuals and help them explore their interests in the vast ocean of technology! However we have a course that is structured at making you equiped with the fundamentals and important techs. The following course plan is a tentative one* and may subject to change. We are also open to teaching courses on request!

Course Plan

  • Python (Intermediate)
  • PHP (Basics)
  • HTML5 (Basics with introduction to commonly used frameworks like Bootstrap and JQuery)
  • Introduction to Android Application Development
  • Introduction to Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Introduction to famous web frameworks (Django, Flask, etc.)
  • Introduction to DBMS with SQL and NoSQL databases
  • Java (Basics)
  • C/C++ (Basics)
  • Approach to Prototyping Applications
  • Introduction to Internet of Things
  • Systematic Design and Approach towards programming
